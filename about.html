About Me

BIO

Fabiano Roggio is an Italian actor from Barletta. Fabiano underwent three years professional actor training at Accademia d’Arte Dramatica Cassiopea (Academy of Dramatic Arts Cassiopea) in Rome. After this training he was selected to attend a Masterclass led by Bernard Hiller where he was one of three selected by Beatrice Kruger to receive a scholarship to attend another Masterclass in LA. After working in Italy for a year, Fabiano moved to Dublin where he enrolled in The Gaiety School of Acting’s Performance Theatre Company (class of 2016). Most recent theatre works include Theatre Lovett: ‘Out and About with Pietr Kloon’ at Kaleidoscope Festival. His recent film credits includes The Man Who Invented Christmas (Parallel Films) and the recently aired Resistance (RTÉ)
